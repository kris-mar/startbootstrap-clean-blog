PhotoApp

A study of Interface and Interface Design

Background

A project from the Daily UI series, I created a catalog of the greatest photographers. I wanted users to be able to located the masters based off of tags such a #landscape, #boxcamera, and #largescale works. Secondly, I wanted users to be able to read a small blurb about their life, follow updates, share their work, and locate their studios. Lastly, and most importantly, I wanted users to be able to access a gallery of their most well known photos. The way I design it makes each photographer's page a single page application and the user could swipe left to right to view other artists. I would have absolutely killed for this app when I taking my history of photography class!

Design

I started with a basic IA to lay out the navigation and define what the website would do and wouldn't do.
