Cloak & Blaster

A rebranding of an Orlando restaurant

Background

When I lived in Orlando during college, I was next to one of the most interesting restaurants, Cloak & Blaster. It's a quirky themed gaming pub that features boardgames, video games, and fandom themed food and drinks. The atmosphere of this place is amazing, it's a great small business which is hard to find in tourist-centric Orlando.

Currently this, is their logo:

Design

As a regular, I really wanted this restaurant to succeed and I know what I could do to help: branding and design. I started with concepts for the logo

I knew we wanted to go with a dragon design, it encapsulated the brand and their decor so well. Eventually, I went with the motif of an Ouroboros. We mocked up the logo in three styles and created the color palette for the rest of the brand

Beyond the logo, I went ahead and created letterheads, marketing coins, and redesigned their menu.
